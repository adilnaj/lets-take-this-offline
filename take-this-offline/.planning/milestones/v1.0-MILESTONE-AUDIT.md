### Integration Gap: NOTF-01 — Push Timezone Not Applied

**File:** `src/app/api/cron/push-dispatch/route.ts:32`
**Problem:** `eq('notify_hour', currentHour)` where `currentHour = new Date().getUTCHours()`. The `push_subscriptions.timezone` column (stored at subscribe time) is never read. A user who selects "8 AM" in the UI receives a push at 8 AM UTC, not 8 AM in their local timezone.
**Fix:** Either (a) convert `currentHour` to each subscriber's local hour using the stored `timezone`, or (b) store `notify_hour` in UTC at subscribe time via `Intl.DateTimeFormat` offset calculation.
