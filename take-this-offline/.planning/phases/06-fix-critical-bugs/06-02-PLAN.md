<objective>
Mount `PracticeSection` on the `/word/[slug]` page so that activities are available on every word permalink, not only on the home page.

Purpose: The v1.0 audit found that `PracticeSection` was wired only to the home page (`/`). Users navigating from the archive to a past word page see the word content but have no way to complete activities, earn points, or maintain their streak. This plan closes that gap by pre-fetching the required server-side data and rendering `PracticeSection` in `WordPage`.

Output: Updated `src/app/word/[slug]/page.tsx` that imports and renders `PracticeSection` with all required props.
</objective>
